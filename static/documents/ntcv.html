Research Interests

My research addresses the intersection of self-care and religion in contemporary North India. The aim of this work is to show how 21st century pursuits of wellbeing not only span religious and medical institutions, but also disrupt the integrity of either as discrete fields. Currently, I am investigating both the patrons of Baba Ramdev and the members of the Gayatri Pariwar. My concerns interface Religion and Medical Anthropology.
